Using WolframAlpha as a statistical calculator

Author: Luc Hens. Last updated: 15 January 2021

The WolframAlpha web page (wolframalpha.com) is the front end of a knowledge engine that (among many other things) is a powerful and user-friendly statistical calculator. On a smartphone or tablet use the mobile version (m.wolframalpha.com), the WolframAlpha app (€3.49, iOS and Android), or the Wolfram Statistics Course Assistant app (€2.29, iOS and Android).
